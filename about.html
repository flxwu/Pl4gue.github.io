Hey!



My name is David,
and I am a just another crazy-minded 16-year-old
teenager attending Schloss Hansenberg, a boarding school
for so called "highly capable students" near Frankfurt, Germany.

“Doubt kills more dreams than failure ever will.” — Suzy Kassem

Doubting your wishes, goals or whatever you dream of happening is the greatest mistake you can make.
At least, that's what I think. I believe that the most important key to both a successful and happy life is following your ambitions and having fun in whatever you are doing.

Apart from school, I use my free time for coding, meeting friends, doing sports and doing other things with computers.

I love exploring the (digital) world and am always interested in learning new things. The most recent example is me doing this website though I had been a firm believer in Web Development being the crappiest thing around. Actually turned out that something I have been hating for so long now excites me with its variety of possibilities. After getting inspired, I started exploring current WebDev technology and ended up learning Django and Node.js among others.

Most of what I currently master regarding software development and computer science is self-educated, although CS class got interesting in 11th grade.
